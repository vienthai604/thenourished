Botanical Heritage

Centuries of
ancestral wisdom.

Axtaxia is a Vietnamese practitioner working with the botanical wisdom from her ancestral roots. The Ancient Beauty Ritual she offers is built around two ingredients that Vietnamese women have trusted for centuries: turmeric and rose.

In Vietnam, turmeric — called nghệ — has been used on the skin for over 2,000 years. Vietnamese women applied fresh turmeric paste after childbirth to help the skin restore, tighten, and heal: it brightens uneven tone, heals scarring, reduces inflammation, and leaves the complexion with a luminosity no other plant can replicate.

Rose — called hoa hồng — was understood in Vietnamese traditional medicine to calm the spirit, cool heat and inflammation, and restore emotional equilibrium. Tender but enduring, rose in this ritual is not decorative. It is devotional.

Around these two ancestral ingredients, Axtaxia weaves a handcrafted botanical blend, prepared fresh before every single session.
